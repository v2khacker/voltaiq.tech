IN-Q-TEL & the CIA

VoltaIQ receives funding from IN-Q-TEL, a venture capital front company for the CIA.

In 2016, Lee Fang of TheIntercept reported that VoltaIQ was among the 38 previously undisclosed companies receiving In-Q-Tel funding. The article was about social media mining and surveillance. However, this document obtained by TheIntercept mentions other technologies as well.

Ethan Leland, the former CTO and a founder of VoltaIQ has a patent entitled System for providing privacy of user information in an online social network with inline editing of privacy levels and customized privacy groups. Eli Leland (also a founder) is the current CTO and all of his research centers around nanotechnology, microscopy and MEMS sensors which are the building blocks of smart dust.
